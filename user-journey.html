AdFlash User Journey

Step 1: Sign Up / Log In

User signs up or logs in to AdFlash to access the creative dashboard.

Step 2: Website Brand Info Extraction

User enters a website URL. AdFlash auto-fetches logo, brand colors, description, and name.

Example: URL: sunnyskincare.com
Brand Name: Sunny Skincare
Colors: #FFD700, #FFB6C1
Tagline: "Naturally radiant"

Step 3: Choose Creative Intent

User selects campaign objective (e.g., awareness, retargeting, sales) which determines the tone and copy style.

Example: Chosen Intent: Retargeting for Repeat Customers

Step 4: Pick Creative Theme

User selects from creative categories like Features & Benefits, Before/After, Testimonial, or Discount Offer.

Example: Selected Theme: Before & After Comparison

Step 5: Template Style Selection

User browses and selects a layout template that best fits the campaign style from our 500+ creative library.

Example: Selected: Side-by-side layout with bold callouts for skincare product

Step 6: AI-Generated Hook & Copy

AI generates multiple hooks (e.g., emotional, problem-solution, transformational) and ad copy using brand tone.

Example: Hook: "See the difference in 7 days"
Headline: "Glow Naturally"
CTA: "Shop the 7-Day Kit"

Step 7: Image Generation

GPT Image 1 creates a design using extracted brand elements and chosen layout. Previewed for approval.

Example: A yellow/pink Before & After skincare ad with branded callout

Why This Ad Works

Element Performance Insight Expected Impact
Hook Emotionally charged, curiosity-driven +37% CTR

Step 8: Review & Download

User downloads high-quality final creative, with options to edit or export to platforms (Meta, Google Ads coming soon).

Example: Downloaded: sunny-ad-creatives-june.png

Step 9: Free Consultation CTA

User sees a non-intrusive prompt offering a 15-min ad consultation. Great for upselling campaign management services.

CTA: "Book Your Free Strategy Call Now"
